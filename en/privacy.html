Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

1. Overview

AI Model Selector respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. Hosted on Vercel, open-source at GitHub.

2. Data We Collect

  • Local Storage: We store preferences (dark mode, favorites, list view) in browser localStorage. Data never leaves your browser.
  • No User Accounts: We do not require registration or collect personal information.
  • No Tracking: We do not use analytics cookies or track individual users.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense: May serve ads and use cookies. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel Hosting: May collect server logs. See Vercel Privacy Policy.

4. API Data

We fetch AI model pricing from public APIs and official pages. We never send your data to AI platforms. All API calls are server-side during site generation.

5. Cookies

We set no first-party cookies. Google AdSense may set third-party cookies. Opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

6. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, delete, or port. You may clear localStorage anytime via browser settings.

7. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.
